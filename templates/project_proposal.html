1. Introduction

In Bangladesh and many other agriculture-based countries, the rice supply chain still heavily relies on manual and traditional processes, which often lead to inefficiencies, lack of transparency, unfair pricing, and delays in delivery. To address these challenges, our team proposes to build a “Rice Supply Chain Management System” – a web-based platform that streamlines the entire journey of rice, starting from rural farmers all the way to the end customer.

Our system will connect key stakeholders such as Dealers, Mill Managers, and Customers, with an Admin overseeing the operations. This digital platform will ensure better communication, transparency in pricing, fair trade, easier transactions, and efficient delivery.

2. Project Objective
  • To digitalize and automate the rice supply chain process.
  • To provide a platform where dealers and mill managers can easily post and negotiate rice/paddy buying and selling.
  • To allow customers to buy rice directly from mill managers.
  • To include a mock payment system for secure and traceable transactions.
  • To add a transportation agreement system between the parties for smooth logistics handling.
3. System Roles and Workflow

Admin:

  • Full control over the website.
  • Can manage users, verify transactions, and handle issues or disputes.

Dealer:

  • Collects paddy from rural farmers.
  • Posts paddy on the website including price, quality, and quantity.
  • Waits for a mill manager to contact and purchase the paddy.

Manager (Mill Manager):

  • Views dealer posts and contacts the dealer for purchase.
  • Makes payments to the dealer via the website.
  • After buying, mills the paddy into rice.
  • Posts the processed rice for sale (with price, quality, quantity).
  • Handles payments from customers and coordinates delivery.

Customer:

  • Can browse rice posts and purchase directly from the manager.
  • Makes mock payments through the system.
  • Coordinates delivery responsibility with the manager.

Transportation Handling:

  • During any transaction, either the dealer or manager (for paddy delivery), or the manager or customer (for rice delivery), will decide who arranges transportation.
  • Based on the agreement, extra transport cost will be added to the total price.
4. Key Features
  • User registration/login for all roles.
  • Posting system for paddy and rice with complete details.
  • Contact and negotiation system between users.
  • Mock payment integration for transactions.
  • Transportation arrangement module with cost calculation.
  • Admin dashboard for complete monitoring and control.
5. Technology Stack
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (with frameworks as needed)
  • Backend: Python (Django)
  • Database: SQLite / PostgreSQL
  • Mock Payment System: Simulated payment gateway using session/transaction records
  • Version Control: Git & GitHub
